Who We Are

Nestled in the heart of Morocco's mystical landscape, we are more than just a destination—we are curators of extraordinary experiences. For over two decades, our luxury desert retreat has welcomed discerning travelers seeking authentic Moroccan hospitality blended with contemporary elegance.

Our story began with a vision to share the timeless beauty of the Agafay Desert while providing unparalleled service and comfort. Today, we continue this legacy, offering bespoke experiences that honor Moroccan traditions while embracing modern luxury.
