WHO ARE WE?

AGAFAY DESERT
MARRAKECH

Come and live a unique and authentic experience out of time

The Agafay desert is a rocky desert located near the city of Marrakech, in Morocco. It is known for its wild beauty and epic landscapes, as well as its dry and arid climate. The Agafay desert is a popular destination for tourists and adventurers, who come to practice activities such as camel rides and buggy rides. You can also discover a variety of plants and animals, such as cacti, acacias and gazelles. The Agafay desert is easily accessible from Marrakech, where you'll find numerous modes of transport to get there.

Everything About The Agafay Experience

Why Morocco?
Morocco offers a unique blend of culture, adventure, and luxury, making it an ideal destination for travelers. Agafay Desert, just 30 minutes from Marrakech, provides an authentic desert experience with stunning landscapes, camel rides, and traditional Berber hospitality—perfect for those seeking an escape without venturing far from the city.
Is drinking tap water in Morocco safe?
Drinking water directly from a tap in Morocco isn't a good idea. However, the Moroccan authorities are concerned about the pollution caused by plastic from bottled water, and many hotels have filtered water for guests which is considered safe to drink.
Visa Requirement?
Most travellers who want to visit Morocco can do so without applying for a visa if your trip will not exceed three months. However, is it essential that all travellers arrive with a passport that is valid for at least six months from the time you enter Morocco?
Can I drink or buy alcohol in Morocco?
Although a predominantly Muslim country, Morocco is not dry. Alcohol is available in restaurants, liquor stores, bars, supermarkets, clubs, hotels and discos. Some Moroccans enjoy a drink although it is disapproved in public places. … Driving under the influence of alcohol is illegal even if you drank just one beer.
What can women wear in Morocco?
In terms of clothing, maxi or midi dresses with sleeves (or at least with the shoulders covered) are perfect, as well as loose trousers and t-shirts. Try to avoid low necklines or anything too figure-hugging, as you might gain some unwanted attention out on the streets.
The level of risk - Is Morocco safe to visit?
According to the 2019 Travel Risk Map, Morocco is the only country to have a low-risk level in North Africa. Algeria and Tunisia are medium risks, while Egypt is high risk.
What is the difference in price between private and group shared desert tour?
Shared tours price is fixed no matter how many people join the tour. Private tours price depends on how many people traveling in your party. The more you are the less expensive you are.
Can we bring luggage on the desert tour?
Yes, you can bring your luggage on our Agafay desert tours. We recommend packing a small backpack for essentials during activities like camel rides. The rest of your luggage can be stored securely in your vehicle or at our lodge for safekeeping.
Can I customize my private tour in Morocco?
Yes, you can! Contact us and we will assist you to make your own tour in Morocco.
