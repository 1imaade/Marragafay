Agafay Desert Basic Pack

Experience the magic of the Agafay Desert with our essential adventure package. Combining thrill and tradition, this is the perfect introduction to Moroccan desert life.

Duration: 4 Hours
Location: Agafay Desert
Guide: Included
Transport: Optional

The Experience

Pickup & Welcome

Your journey begins with a warm welcome and transfer to our luxury camp in the heart of Agafay (if transport selected).

Quad Biking Adventure (1 Hour)

Feel the adrenaline as you race across the lunar landscapes on our powerful 300cc quads. Safety gear and briefing included.

Camel Trek Sunset (20 Min)

Slow down the pace with a traditional camel ride as the sun sets, painting the desert sky in hues of orange and purple.

Dinner & Show

Conclude your evening with a feast of Moroccan delicacies (Tajine, Couscous) accompanied by live Gnaoua music and fire eaters.

What's Included

  • 1 Hour Quad Biking
  • 20 Min Camel Ride
  • Traditional Dinner
  • Live Cultural Show
  • Safety Equipment
  • Mineral Water
Not Included: Hotel Transfer (Extra), Alcoholic Beverages.
