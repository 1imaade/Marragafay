ABOUT MARRAGAFAY.

Marragafay was built on a single refusal: that extraordinary terrain should not require a compromise on safety, vehicle quality, or the depth of experience offered. We operate where the Agafay stone desert meets the Atlas horizon — and we do it to a standard that has no equivalent in Marrakech.

Our Ethos

the marragafay standard

Three principles define every experience we produce. They are not aspirational. They are operational — enforced daily, without exception, by the team that runs every single departure.

01

Uncompromising Safety

Every vehicle in our fleet undergoes a full mechanical inspection before each departure — no exceptions, no shortcuts. Our instructors hold certified safety qualifications and operate under a strict briefing protocol regardless of a client’s stated experience level. In terrain this demanding, preparation is not optional.

03

Verified Local Expertise

Our guides are not hired seasonally. They are native to this landscape — fluent in its hidden corridors, its shifting light, its silence. They lead you through routes that do not appear on any public map, to vantage points that require years of earned knowledge to locate. Authenticity, in this context, is not a marketing word. It is a qualification.

02

The Elite Fleet

We operate the newest, highest-specification ATVs and dune buggies currently available in Agafay. Our fleet is maintained on a rolling replacement schedule — because degraded machinery degrades the experience, and that is a compromise we do not make. When you grip the wheel, you will feel exactly what that means.

experiences rooted in wilderness and luxury

Every expedition is designed as a rare opportunity to create an environment that disconnects you from the false and reconnects you to the things that matter.

Inquiries

frequently asked questions

The questions high-end clients always ask before committing. Answered directly, without reservation.

Are your experiences fully private, or will we be placed in a shared group?

+

All Marragafay experiences are bookable as fully private events — your group, your schedule, your pace. Our Signature and Luxury packages are never shared with other parties unless you explicitly request a shared departure at the time of booking. If you have four guests, the guide, the vehicles, and the dinner table belong to your four guests alone. That is the default. It is not an upgrade.

What is the actual condition of your ATV and buggy fleet?

+

Our fleet is the newest in operation across Agafay, maintained on a rolling replacement schedule and subject to a full mechanical inspection before every single departure. If a vehicle does not pass pre-departure inspection, it does not leave the compound — and an equivalent replacement is provided with no delay to your itinerary. We are not in the business of running aging machinery because it is cheaper to maintain. The fleet is a core part of the experience, and it is treated accordingly.

Do you handle hotel transfers directly from Marrakech?

+

Yes. Private door-to-door transfer from your hotel or riad in Marrakech is included in all Signature and Luxury packages. Our driver will confirm your pickup location, entrance name, and exact time the evening prior — and will arrive within a two-minute window of the scheduled departure. If your itinerary changes on the day, notify us via WhatsApp and we will adjust without penalty.

Can the desert dinner accommodate specific dietary requirements — allergies, religious restrictions, or personal preferences?

+

Yes, in full. When you book, you will be asked to declare any dietary requirements — allergies (including severe and anaphylactic), religious dietary laws, vegetarian or vegan preferences, or any other restrictions. These are communicated directly to our camp chef and confirmed 24 hours before your arrival. We do not offer a modified version of a standard menu. We prepare for your actual requirements. If you have a complex medical condition requiring specific preparation, contact us directly before booking so we can confirm our capacity to accommodate it correctly.
