WHO ARE WE?

AGAFAY DESERT
MARRAKECH

Come and live a unique and authentic experience out of time

The Agafay desert is a rocky desert located near the city of Marrakech, in Morocco. It is known for its wild beauty and epic landscapes, as well as its dry and arid climate. The Agafay desert is a popular destination for tourists and adventurers, who come to practice activities such as camel rides and buggy rides. You can also discover a variety of plants and animals, such as cacti, acacias and gazelles. The Agafay desert is easily accessible from Marrakech, where you'll find numerous modes of transport to get there.

Why Choose Agafay Marrakech

Agafay Marrakech Desert

Your Perfect Place To Discover Agafay

The Agafay Desert is known for its wild beauty and epic landscapes. Its sand dunes, rocks and hills offer a unique and impressive setting. The Agafay Desert is also rich in biodiversity, with a variety of plants and animals that live there, such as cacti, trees and gazelles.

The brightness and warmth of the desert, as well as the coolness of the night, also contribute to its beauty. The Agafay Desert is an ideal destination for those seeking to recharge and reconnect with nature.
