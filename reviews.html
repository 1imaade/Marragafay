FAQ

Everything About The Agafay Experience

Why Morocco?
Morocco offers a unique blend of culture, adventure, and luxury. Agafay Desert provides an authentic desert experience just 30 minutes from Marrakech.
Is drinking tap water safe?
Bottled water is recommended. Many hotels have filtered water for guests which is safe to drink.
Visa requirements?
Most travelers can visit Morocco without a visa for up to 3 months. Passport must be valid for 6 months from entry.
Can I customize my tour?
Yes! Contact us and we will assist you to create your perfect tour in Morocco.
Is Morocco safe to visit?
According to the 2019 Travel Risk Map, Morocco is the only country with low-risk level in North Africa.
